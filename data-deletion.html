Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: 2026-05-06

Adonis is a self-hosted operator tool. Data is stored locally on the operator's machine, not in any cloud or third-party service.

How to request deletion

  1. Open an issue on the project's GitHub repository titled Data deletion request with the email or page name you want removed.
  2. The operator will purge your tokens, drafts, and account metadata from the local database within 7 days.
  3. You will receive a confirmation reply once the deletion is complete.

What gets deleted

  • OAuth tokens for your connected Facebook / Instagram accounts
  • Post drafts that reference your accounts
  • Cached account metadata (page names, IDs)

What does not need deletion

We do not maintain copies in any third-party cloud or analytics service. Once removed locally, no further action is required.
