Analytics for the content you make.

Adonis Ads Intelligence is an internal analytics workspace that helps creators understand how their own social media content performs across platforms — without the noise of native dashboards.

Own-Content Insights

Connect your TikTok, Facebook, or Instagram account and pull engagement metrics for posts you authored.

Unified Dashboard

One view across platforms — views, likes, shares, comments, and sponsorship signals normalized into a shared schema.

Privacy by Design

Access is granted via OAuth on each platform. You can disconnect at any time and your data is removed.

How It Works

Adonis connects to social platforms through their official developer APIs. When you authorize the app on a platform (for example, TikTok via Login Kit), Adonis receives a scoped access token that can read your profile and the metadata of videos you have published.

The data is processed in your private workspace. Adonis does not share, sell, or expose your data to other users of the tool.

Supported Platforms

  • TikTok — via Login Kit and Display API (profile, video list, engagement metrics)
  • Facebook — via Graph API (page insights and post performance)
  • Instagram — via Graph API (business and creator accounts)
