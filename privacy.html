Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what data Adonis Ads Intelligence (the “Service”) collects, how it is used, and what choices you have. We aim to collect only what we need and to be transparent about how it is processed.

1. Who We Are

Adonis Ads Intelligence is operated by Radu Roman (the “Operator”), based in Romania. The Operator acts as the data controller for the personal data described in this policy.

Contact: raduroman94@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Data

When you create an Adonis account, we collect the email address you provide and, optionally, a display name. We store a password hash, not your password itself.

2.2 Data from Connected Social Media Platforms

When you connect a social media account (for example, via TikTok’s Login Kit), the platform issues an access token to the Service after you grant consent. Using that token and only within the scopes you authorized, the Service may retrieve:

  • Profile information: open ID, display name, avatar URL, bio, follower and following counts, total likes received, and total video count.
  • Content metadata: identifiers, titles, descriptions, captions, creation timestamps, durations, cover images, share URLs, and embed information for content you have published.
  • Engagement metrics: view counts, like counts, comment counts, and share counts for content you have published.

The Service does not request scopes that would let it post on your behalf, message other users, or read content authored by other users that is not publicly available.

2.3 Technical Data

To operate the Service securely, we record limited technical information such as IP address, browser type, request timestamps, and error logs. These logs are retained for a short period and used only for debugging, abuse prevention, and security.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data described above to:

  • Display analytics about content you have published on connected platforms.
  • Authenticate you and keep your session active.
  • Refresh expired access tokens on your behalf, so you do not have to re-authorize on every visit.
  • Detect abuse, investigate incidents, and keep the Service secure.
  • Comply with legal obligations and platform requirements (including TikTok’s Developer Terms of Service).

We do not use your data to train machine-learning models, to build advertising profiles, or for any purpose unrelated to providing the Service to you.

4. Legal Bases (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, our legal bases for processing are:

  • Consent — for connecting a social media account and retrieving its data via OAuth. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Contract — processing necessary to provide the Service you signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests — for security, abuse prevention, and improving the Service, balanced against your rights.
  • Legal obligation — where applicable law or platform terms require us to retain or disclose data.

5. Sharing of Data

We do not sell your personal data and we do not share it with third parties for marketing. We share data only with the limited categories of recipients required to operate the Service:

  • Infrastructure providers that host the Service and its databases, under data processing agreements.
  • Social media platforms — when the Service calls their APIs on your behalf, the request includes the access token they issued for you.
  • Authorities — when required to do so by valid legal process.

6. Data Retention

We keep your account data and the analytics retrieved from connected platforms for as long as you keep your account active. If you disconnect a social media account, the access token is revoked locally and the cached data for that connection is deleted within 30 days. If you delete your Adonis account, all associated data is deleted within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Security

Access tokens are stored encrypted at rest. Communication between your browser, the Service, and connected platforms uses TLS. Access to production systems is limited and logged. No system is perfectly secure, so we encourage you to use a strong unique password and to revoke connections you no longer use.

8. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Delete your data (the “right to be forgotten”).
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent for OAuth connections at any time, both within the Service and on the connected platform.
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (in Romania: ANSPDCP, www.dataprotection.ro).

To exercise any of these rights, write to raduroman94@gmail.com. We respond within 30 days.

9. International Transfers

The Service may use infrastructure providers located outside the European Economic Area. When data is transferred outside the EEA, we rely on lawful transfer mechanisms such as the European Commission’s Standard Contractual Clauses.

10. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 18 and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you believe a minor has provided data to the Service, contact us and we will delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

12. Contact

For privacy questions, data requests, or anything else covered by this policy, contact raduroman94@gmail.com.
