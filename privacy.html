Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-06

Adonis is a self-hosted operator tool used by a single team to schedule social-media content. The application stores only the minimum data required to operate the service for the authenticated operator: connected Facebook Pages metadata, Instagram business account info, post drafts, and OAuth tokens.

Data we store

  • OAuth access tokens for connected Facebook / Instagram accounts
  • Post drafts and schedule queue (text + media references)
  • Page / account names and IDs returned by Meta

Data we do NOT collect

  • End-user analytics or telemetry
  • Content from your friends, followers, or third parties

Sharing

We do not share data with third parties. Tokens stay on the operator's local instance and are encrypted at rest.

Deletion

To request deletion of the data we hold for your account, see the Data Deletion Instructions.

Contact
